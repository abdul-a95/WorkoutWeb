Frequently Asked Questions

01 What should I use WorkoutWeb for?

You can use WorkoutWeb for all things fitness and health! As long as you respect all other users and their opinions, you will be able to enjoy your time as a memeber! Feel free to to comment on posts, share your opinions, and get the latest tips and tricks.

02 Why can't I see the username of the person who posted?

You can only see the username of the poster or commenter if you're logged into your account. If you don't have an account yet, you can create one by clicking on the "sign-up" button in the top right corner.
